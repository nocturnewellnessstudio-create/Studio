Bookings & Memberships

Classes can be booked through our Member App, via our website after logging in, or by contacting us on WhatsApp. We recommend booking at least 24–48 hours in advance as classes fill up quickly.

Cancellations made more than 4 hours before the class start time are fully refunded to your account. Late cancellations (within 4 hours) will forfeit the session credit. No-shows also forfeit the session.

Memberships are individual and non-transferable. Each person must have their own account. Monthly members do receive guest passes which allow bringing friends to class.
