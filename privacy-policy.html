How we collect, use, and protect your data

At Nocturne Wellness Studio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or contact us through our forms:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Preferred topic or service enquiry
  • Any message or details you submit through the contact form

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and messages
  • Provide information about classes, packages, and services
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Communicate with you regarding bookings or general concerns

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with trusted service providers when necessary to operate our website, receive form submissions, or provide support.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or basic analytics tools to help us understand how visitors use the site, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have submitted to us, subject to applicable laws and reasonable verification.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through our Contact Page.
